# Phase 1/2 decision

1. Keep the isolated prototype as the candidate mathematical direction.
2. Do not call it the formal shared core until page-scale conversions, legacy compatibility, and contact regressions are audited.
3. Do not tune Magnus, restitution, friction, blend, or preset geometry from these prototype checks.
4. The next red-line proposal would replace duplicated page contact/flight paths with this kernel in `shared-physics-core.js`, then update the page adapters. That proposal requires explicit authorization before implementation.
